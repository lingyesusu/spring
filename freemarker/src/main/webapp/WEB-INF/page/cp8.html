内建函数

    1,字符串内建函数

    <#list "a|b|c|d"?split("|") as item>
  • ${item}

    • 2,字符串内建函数

    <#assign var1="01/03/2017"?date("MM/dd/yyyy")/> <#assign var2="15:05:30"?time("HH:mm:ss")/> <#assign var3="2016-12-31 03:05PM"?datetime("yyyy-MM-dd hh:mm")/>
  • ${var1}
  • ${var2}
  • ${var3}

    • 3,数字类型内建函数

    <#assign numVar1=314.5662/>
  • ${numVar1?string("0.##")}
  • ${numVar1?round}

    • 4,list内建函数

    <#assign listVar1=[1,2,3,4,11,12,13,14,21,22,23,24]/>
  • ${listVar1?chunk(4)?size}
    • <#list listVar1?chunk(4)?last as item>
  • ${item}

    • 5,其他内建函数

    <#assign sVar='hello'/>
  • ${sVar?is_number?string('yes','no')}
  • ${sVar?is_string?string('yes','no')}
  • ${sVar?has_content?string('yes','no')}
  • ${"1"+"2"}
  • ${("1"+"2")?eval}
  • ${"1+2"?eval}
